Property Description

Potential family compound! Secluded custom 5,895sf main home, 1bd/1ba granny flat over the garage & 3bd/2ba manufactured home all set on 28 wooded acres. Main home has a great room floor plan showcasing vaulted ceilings, recessed lighting, surround sound throughout the house & stone flooring. Dazzling granite kitchen features a dumb waiter, butler's pantry & double oven. Take pleasure in having a master suite & 3 junior masters perfect for guests. Travel downstairs & you will find a living space, separate entrance, bedroom, full bath, wood stove, laundry room & huge storage area. A breezeway takes you to the 1bd/1ba granny flat over the 3 car garage. Garage also features a greenhouse room, laundry room & workshop w/full bath. Off the grid manufactured home features solar panels w/back up generator, tankless hot water heater, outdoor dog bath, remodeled kitchen w/custom concrete countertops & more. Acreage includes a full basketball court, fenced garden, solar, local views & more.
