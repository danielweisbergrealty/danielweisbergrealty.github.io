About

After growing up in Toronto, Canada and completing his bachelor's in Computer Engineering, Daniel has lived and worked in several cities around the world, including Seattle, San Francisco, and Weston. He has always had a passion for real estate, and after receiving his license in Florida, he decided to pursue a career in the field. He works as a part-time realtor and a full-time software engineer at Microsoft.

Florida's real estate market has been growing in recent years, with demand for both residential and commercial properties increasing. Daniel has extensive knowledge of the local market and is able to find his clients the best possible deals by using data points such as crime statistics, school quality, ROI, rental estimates, and the effective life of depreciating assets. In addition to his analytical skills, Daniel is also known for his ability to listen carefully to the unique needs, desires, and goals of each of his clients. This personal approach, combined with his deep understanding of the market, has helped him to be an effective agent.

As an investor himself, Daniel has a track record of success in finding and flipping properties. One of his most notable transactions was when he purchased a property in Tampa without ever seeing it, while still living in Seattle. The day after closing, he flew to the property and completed a full refresh, and had the house rented out by the end of the month.

If you are an investor looking for a good deal or a first-time homebuyer in search of your dream home, don't hesitate to reach out to Daniel. He would be happy to discuss how he can help you achieve your real estate goals. Contact him today to get started!
